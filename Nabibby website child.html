What are they?

Children are young individuals who are in the early stages of life and development. They typically range from infancy to adolescence, a period marked by rapid growth, learning, and emotional, physical, and cognitive development. Children are curious, full of energy, and eager to explore the world around them. They learn through play, social interaction, and education, gradually acquiring skills, knowledge, and independence. Children are also highly influenced by their environment, including family, peers, and culture, which shape their values and understanding of the world. As they grow, children begin to form their identities, developing unique personalities and perspectives.
